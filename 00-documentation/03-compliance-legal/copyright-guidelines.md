# Copyright Guidelines for Istabraq

## 1. What Can Be Uploaded

### ✅ Allowed:
- Public domain works (published before 1928)
- Works with explicit permission from copyright holder
- Creative Commons licensed works
- User's own original content

### ❌ Not Allowed:
- Copyrighted works without permission
- Modern publications (post-1928) without proof of permission
- Pirated content
- Content with unclear copyright status

## 2. Required Information for Uploads
For each upload, provide:
- Title and author
- Publication year
- Copyright status (Public Domain, CC-BY, Permission Granted)
- Proof of permission (if applicable)
- Original source/location

## 3. Takedown Process
If you believe content infringes copyright:
1. Visit https://istabraq.org/takedown
2. Provide:
   - Your contact information
   - Exact URL of infringing content
   - Proof of copyright ownership
   - Reason for request
3. We will:
   - Acknowledge receipt within 48 hours
   - Investigate within 7 days
   - Remove if valid
   - Notify uploader

## 4. Fair Use Considerations
We allow scholarly use including:
- Textual analysis
- Research purposes
- Educational use
- Preservation of cultural heritage

## 5. Attribution Requirements
All content must include:
- Original author
- Source (if known)
- Uploader name
- License information
